About Us:

Shalom.Co is a gripping and unconventional clothing brand based in Surakarta since 2001. Represents not only a more refined and forward-thinking brand, we reflect our each issues like an abstract arts and every articles is like a art we create. Driven by the dream-quest of demon force that bore black mass hysteria, a carnal beast, living in a cosmos that is indifferent to our existence. Shalom.Co is dangerous flame of brand that seemed lost for many years and that now once again has been set loose upon everyday society & to decipher the world objectively. Four young devils were brought together by our passion for art. Our first love has always been and will always be on art, we try to give back to our roots through the products and opportunities we provide through the company. We remains extremely involved in the production and direction of Shalom.Co, maintaining the respect over communities we continue to support our friends, which helps us fuel and inspire our original vision. Our goal for Shalom.Co is to evoke emotions then create topics of discussion through our designs and boldly stated our idea at the hand of youth culture.
